I am a Ph.D. candidate pursuing Computational Neuroscience in the Center for Theoretical Neuroscience and Zuckerman Mind Brain Behavior Institute at Columbia University. I develop neuroscience-inspired AI systems and apply ML to understand human brains (Neuro ↔ AI).

I graduated from the University of Washington, Seattle (UW), as part of the NIH-funded Computational Neuroscience Training Program, with a B.S. in Applied & Computational Mathematics and a B.A. in Psychology with Honors. I still maintain close collaborations and affliations with Columbia Department of Systems Biology and UW Department of Applied Mathematics.
